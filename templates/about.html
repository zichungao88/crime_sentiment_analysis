This is the crime data in Howard County, Maryland that I have collected from HCPD from February to March 2022.
The process involves using BeautifulSoup to extract the data and displaying it on this Flask website.
The goal is to inform the public about crime rates, trends, and social sentiment regarding crimes.

Data Source: https://www.howardcountymd.gov/police/newsroom
Source Code: https://github.com/zichungao88/flask_crime_data
Note: This website is a work in progress. More data will be updated and displayed in the future accordingly.
